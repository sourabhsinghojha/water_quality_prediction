About AquaScan

AquaScan is an advanced water quality analysis platform that uses an optimized Machine Learning model to determine if water is potable. By analyzing key indicators like pH, TDS, turbidity, chloramines and more, AquaScan provides instant results with high accuracy.

Our mission is to make water testing accessible, fast, and reliable for households, industries, and researchers.
